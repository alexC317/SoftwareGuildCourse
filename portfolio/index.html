About Me

Hi! My name is Alex, I am an aspiring web developer born and raised in Queens, New York. Computers have long been an interest of mine; when I was in Kindergarten, I once asked a teacher when we were going to work with computers!

My path to becoming a web developer began at the Bronx High School of Science, when I took a few programming classes, wanting to learn how to code. I grew quickly to appreciate Java and the concepts behind object-oriented programming. From there, I majored in Computer Science at Hunter College, receiving my Bachelor's Degree. Now I am currently enrolled in the Java-based Web Development course at the LaGuardia TechHire program!
